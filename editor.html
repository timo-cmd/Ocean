Ocean

- A lightweight text editor

Supporting dark-light themes for day and night use.

Auto-save is enabled by default so when you close the window, it will be saved and can be edited after a while again.

Ocean is built and powered by the theme used in Lavendel. The project is licenced and mantained by Timo Sarkar.

For any bug-report or feature-request, open an issue on GitHub.
